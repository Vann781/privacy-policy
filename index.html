Privacy Policy

Naam Jap

A quiet, private space for your daily japa.
Last updated · 13 June 2026

In plain words

Naam Jap was made for one purpose — to help you keep count while you chant. It has no login, it never asks who you are, and it sends nothing about you to us or to anyone else. The only thing it remembers is how many times you have chanted, and that number stays on your phone.

No account needed No tracking or ads Stays on your device
01

About this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Naam Jap mobile application (“Naam Jap”, “the app”, “we”, “us”, “our”) handles information. Naam Jap is published by Vayu Mishra.

By downloading or using the app, you agree to the practices described on this page. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the app.

02

Information we collect

In short: no personal information. Naam Jap does not collect, request, or store your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal or identifying information.

So that your practice is remembered between sessions, the app saves a small amount of non-personal information on your device only:

  • Your jap count for each deity — how many repetitions you have completed and how many malas (108 repetitions) you have finished.
  • Your current session preferences, such as the Auto Play speed you last chose.

This is stored using your operating system’s standard on-device app storage. It is simply a number kept for each deity, and it is never transmitted to us or to any third party.

03

Information we do not collect

To be completely clear, the app does not collect or have access to any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers — name, email, phone number, or user accounts
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Your contacts, calendar, photos, files, or microphone input
  • Advertising identifiers or any identifiers used for tracking
  • Usage analytics, behavioural data, or crash-tracking tied to you
  • Payment or financial information

Naam Jap contains no advertising and no analytics services.

04

How your device’s features are used

To act as a chanting companion, the app uses a few features that run on your own device:

  • Text-to-speech. When you tap to count, the app asks your device’s built-in text-to-speech engine to pronounce the deity’s name (for example, “Radha”). Only that short word is passed to the engine. How the speech is produced is governed by your device’s operating system and its text-to-speech settings.
  • Audio playback. In Auto Play mode, the app plays pre-recorded chant audio that is bundled inside the app itself. Nothing is streamed or downloaded.
  • Vibration. The app may produce a gentle vibration as tactile feedback when you tap.

The app does not use your camera, microphone, or any recording feature.

05

Internet and offline use

Naam Jap is designed to work fully offline. Counting, the spoken name on each tap, and Auto Play all work without an internet connection and do not send your information over the network. We do not operate servers that receive your data, because the app does not transmit any.

06

Third-party services and sharing

The app does not integrate advertising networks, analytics services, social-media tools, or other third-party tracking software. We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your information to third parties, because we do not collect it.

Your device’s text-to-speech and audio output are provided by your operating system and are subject to the policies of your device manufacturer and operating-system provider (for example, Google for Android devices).

07

Where your data lives, and how to remove it

Because everything Naam Jap remembers stays on your device, you are fully in control of it:

  • Reset a single count. The reset button on a deity’s screen clears that deity’s count back to zero. Other deities are not affected.
  • Remove everything. Uninstalling the app deletes all data it has stored on your device, including every jap count.

We keep no backups of your data and cannot recover counts once they have been reset or the app has been uninstalled.

08

Children’s privacy

Naam Jap is suitable for users of all ages. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where local law applies). Since no personal information is collected from any user, no data is gathered from children either. If you have any concern about a child’s use of the app, please contact us using the details below.

09

App permissions

Depending on your device and operating-system version, the app may use:

  • Vibration — to provide gentle tap feedback.
  • Audio output — to play chant audio and spoken names through your speaker or headphones.

Naam Jap does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, personal files, or any account on your device. You can review and manage app permissions at any time in your device settings.

10

Google Play Data safety

In line with Google Play’s Data safety section, Naam Jap declares that no user data is collected and no user data is shared with third parties. Information such as your jap count is stored only on your device and is never sent off the device.

When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page, and meaningful changes will appear in a future version of the app. Continuing to use the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
